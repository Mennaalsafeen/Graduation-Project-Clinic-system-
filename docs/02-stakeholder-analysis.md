# Stakeholder Analysis

## Stakeholders Overview
Stakeholders are individuals or groups who are involved in or affected by the Dental Clinic Appointment Management System.

| Stakeholder | Role | Interest / Concern | Involvement Level |
|------------|------|--------------------|------------------|
| Admin / Receptionist | Manages clinic users and appointments | Needs a simple way to organize bookings and schedules | High |
| Dentist | Views and manages assigned appointments | Needs access to daily schedule and patient appointment details | High |
| Patient | Books and manages appointments | Needs a fast and easy booking experience | High |
| Developer Team | Builds and maintains the system | Needs clear requirements and stable scope | Medium |

## Stakeholder Details
### Admin / Receptionist
- Manages users, appointments, and system settings.
- Ensures the clinic schedule is organized.

### Dentist
- Views upcoming appointments.
- Updates availability and manages daily schedule.

### Patient
- Registers and logs in.
- Books, updates, and cancels appointments.
- Views appointment history.

### Developer Team
- Develops the system features.
- Fixes bugs and maintains documentation.
