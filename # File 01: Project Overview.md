# File 01: Project Overview

## 1. Project Name & Description
**Project Name:** Dental Clinic Appointment Management System

**Description:**
An integrated digital solution designed to streamline and automate the daily operations of dental clinics. This system aims to replace traditional paper-based management with a smart digital interface that connects patients, doctors, and receptionists. It focuses on optimizing scheduling, archiving electronic health records (EHR), and improving the overall patient experience through a clean, responsive interface, while providing comprehensive administrative insights into clinic performance.

---

## 2. Main Users
The system identifies four primary roles to ensure a seamless workflow:
1. **Admin:** Has full authority to manage user accounts, permissions, and monitor comprehensive financial and administrative reports.
2. **Doctor:** Focuses on managing their personal schedule, accessing patient medical histories, documenting diagnoses, and updating treatment plans.
3. **Receptionist:** Acts as the central hub; manages physical check-ins, registers new patients, organizes daily flow, and processes billing.
4. **Patient:** Can browse available time slots, book or cancel appointments, and view their personal visit history and medical profile.

---

## 3. Main Goal
To develop a technically organized environment that eliminates appointment conflicts and provides instant access to patient records and X-rays. This increases the speed of medical service delivery, reduces human error in scheduling, and enhances the management of both medical and financial resources.

---

## 4. Core Features List
The system consists of 9 essential features:

1. **Smart Dashboard:** Provides a real-time summary of daily appointments, urgent alerts, and quick statistics on patient volume.
2. **Advanced Appointment Management:** A flexible scheduling engine that prevents time-slot overlapping and allows for instant booking, rescheduling, and cancellation.
3. **EHR & Visual Dental Chart:** A digital record for every patient featuring an interactive visual dental map to precisely mark pain points or medical procedures.
4. **Automated Notification System:** Sends automatic reminders via the platform to patients and doctors regarding upcoming appointments or schedule changes.
5. **Rapid Patient Search:** Enables staff to locate any patient record within seconds using name, phone number, or unique National ID.
6. **Services & Pricing Management:** Allows the clinic to define and categorize treatment types (e.g., Orthodontics, Surgery, Cosmetic) and link each to its specific cost.
7. **Billing & Payments:** Generates accurate digital invoices, tracks paid and outstanding balances, and manages different payment methods.
8. **Patient Feedback System:** A dedicated module for patients to rate their visit and provide feedback, helping management improve service quality (E-Business integration).
9. **Resource & Inventory Management:** Tracks the consumption of essential dental tools and materials, notifying the admin when stock levels are low.
