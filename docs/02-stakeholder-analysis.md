# 02. Stakeholder Analysis

# Dental Clinic Appointment Management System
## 👥 Stakeholders Overview

Stakeholders are individuals or groups who are involved in or affected by the system.

| Stakeholder | Role | Interest / Concern | Involvement Level |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Admin / Receptionist** | Manages clinic users and appointments | Needs a simple way to organize bookings and schedules | **High** |
| **Dentist** | Views and manages assigned appointments | Needs access to daily schedule and patient details | **High** |
| **Patient** | Books and manages appointments | Needs a fast and easy booking experience | **High** |
| **Developer Team** | Builds and maintains the system | Needs clear requirements and stable scope | **Medium** |

---

## 📝 Stakeholder Details

### 🛠 Admin / Receptionist
* **Responsibilities:**
    * Manage users (staff and patients).
    * Oversee system settings and configurations.
    * Ensure the clinic schedule is organized and conflict-free.

### 🩺 Dentist
* **Responsibilities:**
    * View real-time upcoming appointments.
    * Update personal availability and working hours.
    * Manage the flow of the daily schedule.

### 👤 Patient
* **Responsibilities:**
    * Account registration and secure login.
    * Self-service booking, updating, and canceling appointments.
    * Accessing personal appointment history.

### 💻 Developer Team
* **Responsibilities:**
    * Develop and implement system features.
    * Identify and fix technical bugs.
    * Maintain technical documentation and system stability.
